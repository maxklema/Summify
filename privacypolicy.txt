Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Summify ("I", "my") collects, uses, and shares information collected from users ("you" or "your") of the Summify Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

When you use the Summify Chrome extension, we may collect the following types of information:

Website Content: We may collect website content, including text, images, sounds, videos, or hyperlinks, from the web pages you visit for the purpose of summarization within the Summify extension.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Summarization: We use the collected website content to generate summaries within the Summify extension to enhance your browsing experience.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell or transfer your information to third parties. The information we collect is used solely to provide the summarization functionality within the Summify extension.

Your Choices

You can choose to disable or uninstall the Summify extension at any time. This will stop the collection of website content by the extension.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
